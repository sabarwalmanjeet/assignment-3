NVIDIA: A Technology Giant Shaping the Future of Computing

NVIDIA is a technology powerhouse known for its pioneering work in graphics processing units (GPUs), artificial intelligence (AI), and data center solutions. Since its founding in 1993 by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem, the company has transformed from a niche graphics card maker into a global leader in high-performance computing. Today, NVIDIA is at the heart of some of the most exciting and disruptive technologies shaping industries such as gaming, AI, cloud computing, and automotive.
