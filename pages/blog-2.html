Intel: Shaping the Future of Computing

Intel Corporation, founded in 1968 by Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore, is one of the most influential technology companies in the world. Known primarily for designing and manufacturing microprocessors, Intel has played a pivotal role in driving the evolution of personal computing, enterprise solutions, and emerging technologies. Over the decades, the company has helped shape the digital age through its innovations, transforming industries from consumer electronics and data centers to autonomous vehicles and artificial intelligence.
